Transportation

Sunny Coast Internation Airport provides different options of transportation for travellers to choose from. Passengers can travel to the city center, different tourist attractions and hotels within one hour. Different transportation options have their own price and travel time, please check the options below to find the best service that suits your needs.

Click here to see the transportation map to an from the airport

Airport Express Train



The fastest way to travel between the airport and CBD, it provides comfortable seating, giving passengers a relaxing journey.

Schedule: depart every 15 mins from 06:00 to 00:00 daily

Price:
- Adult: $20
- Child: $10
- Concession: $15

*Passenger can buy the train pass for $30 (include to & from airport)

Public Buses



There are approximately 20 bus routes that are connected to the airport. It provides a convenient way to travel to different places in the city. Also, it is the cheapest transport option!

Schedule: depart every 5 mins from 05:00 to 00:00 daily

Price:
- Adult: $5
- Child: $2
- Concession: $2

Taxis



Travelling by taxis provide a point to point service to and form the airport. Passengers can walk to to the taxi terminal which is located next to the Arrivals Hall.

Average price to:
- CDB: $40
- Southbank: $35
- Wonderland: $60
