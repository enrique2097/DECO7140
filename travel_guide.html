Arrival Procedures



To ensure a smooth and efficient arrival, you can browse all the essential information that you need to know after landing at Sunny Coast International Airport.

1. Passport Control



Before arriving in the immigration hall, make sure to have the following travel documents ready:

- A valid passsport
- Declaration form
- Arrival Card
- Covid-19 vaccination certificate

2. Baggage Claim



After passing through the immigration hall, you will enter the bagge claim area. Check the information board in the middle of the hall to see which carousel has been assigned to your flight
