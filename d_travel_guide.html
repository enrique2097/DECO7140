Departures Procedures



Below are step-by-step departure instructions for you to enjoy a quick and smooth journey to your boarding gate.

1. Check-in



After entering the airport, proceed to the designated check-in counter for your airline. Make sure to have all your travel documents ready before checking in, including:

- A valid passsport
- Air ticket
- Any required visas


Reminder: Please early to allow sufficient time for passport control and security procedures

2. Baggage



Travellers are advised to check the baggage requirements according to the airlines’s security guidelines

Baggage Drop-off: Proceed to the baggage drop off counter for checking in your bags

Hand Baggage: Remind that your baggage’s size must not exceed 56cm x 36cm x 23cm



Baggage Measureing Gauge

According to the cabin baggage guidelines set by the Airline Operators Committee, cabin baggage carried onboard an aircraft must not exceed 56cm x 36cm x 23 cm (22” x 14” x 9”).



To ensure your cabin baggage meets the guidelines, you can use one of the many measuring gauges found at each check-in aisle in the Check-in Hall and at the Immigration Hall entrances of Departures Level. Please contact your airline should you need any assistance.

3. Security Check



There will be security check when entering the restricted area of the airport, your hand baggage will be inspected and a body scan will be conducted. Please follow the instructions given by our staffs

4. Immigration



Before arriving in the immigration hall, make sure to have the following travel documents ready:

- A valid passsport
- Departure card (for foreigners)
- Other travel documents

5. Boarding



Please arrive at your boading gate 30 minutes before the departure time. Your boarding gate number is printed on your boading pass, you can also check the information boards for the latest status of your flight.



Enjoy your flight!
